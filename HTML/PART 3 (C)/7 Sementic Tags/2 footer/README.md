## 🎯 Topics Covered

### 1. **Footer Tag Basics**
`<footer>` ek semantic tag hai jo page ke sabse neeche ya kisi specific section ke aakhir mein lagaya jata hai. Isme aam taur par copyright info, author details, aur contact links hote hain. Ye `<div>` tag ka ek behtar, semantic alternative hai for footers.

```html
<footer>
    <p>&copy; 2026 My Website</p>
    <p>Contact: email@example.com</p>
</footer>
```

### 2. **Header aur Footer ek sath**
Is practice mein humne dekha ke kaise ek webpage ke top par `<header>` aur bottom par `<footer>` laga kar ek proper page ka structure banaya jata hai.

---

## 📝 Summary
Footer tag search engines aur screen readers ko batata hai ke yahan par is page ya section ka main content khatam ho gaya hai aur ye additional closing information hai. Isko `<article>` ya `<section>` ke andar bhi as a footer use kiya ja sakta hai.
